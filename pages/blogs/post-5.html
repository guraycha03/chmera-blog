10 Useful Websites Every Student Should Know

Student Life • by Charisse Guray • Sept 12, 2025

Being a student means juggling classes, assignments, projects, and personal life. Here’s a curated list of websites that can help you study smarter, organize your work, and even relax:

1. Khan Academy

Free lessons on math, science, and more. Great for brushing up on topics or learning something new at your own pace.

2. Coursera

Online courses from top universities. Learn new skills, earn certificates, and explore areas outside your curriculum.

3. Canva

Design presentations, posters, or social media graphics effortlessly. Perfect for student projects and assignments.

4. Quizlet

Create flashcards, quizzes, and study guides. Ideal for exam preparation and memory retention.

5. Trello

Organize tasks, track deadlines, and collaborate with peers. Helps keep school projects on track.

6. Notion

All-in-one workspace for notes, projects, and schedules. Customize it to fit your study workflow.

7. Library Genesis

Access textbooks, articles, and academic papers. A helpful resource for research and self-study.

8. Typing.com

Improve your typing speed and accuracy with free lessons. Saves time when writing essays or notes.

9. RescueTime

Track time spent on apps and websites. Helps students stay focused and productive.

10. Calm

Guided meditations, sleep stories, and relaxation exercises. Essential for stress relief and mental well-being.

These tools can make student life a little easier and more productive. Which one will you try first?
